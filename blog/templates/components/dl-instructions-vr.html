VR - Oculus Go / Quest Instructions VR

  1. Download and install Sidequest (Advanced Installer): https://sidequestvr.com/setup-howto
  2. Download the ENP apk below.
  3. Connect the Oculus Quest headset to your computer via a USB C cable.
  4. Turn on the headset.
  5. Select “Allow Computer to Access Files.
    6. ”
  6. Open “Sidequest” from your PC."
  7. Select the “Install” icon in the upper right hand corner.
  8. Select the ENP or product apk to install.
  9. Put on the headset.
  10. Select “Unknown Sources” in the home screen applications menu.
  11. Find the Empower Now Program or product apk.
  12. Select the application.

VR - Steam Instructions VR

  1. Launch the Steam app on your VR ready Desktop or Laptop.
  2. In the top navigation menu, under "Games" select Activate a product on Steam.
  3. Click "Next" to be prompted to enter the product code.
  4. Enter the redemption code provided by Sisu VR with your purchase and click "Next."
  5. You will be prompted to download and install the app.
