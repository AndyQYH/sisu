Frequently Asked Questions
How does Sisu VR’s training work?

The Empower Now Program is an immersive anti-harassment training experience based on real field cases. The user experiences the training from multiple perspectives (i.e. victim, perpetrator, and observer) and educates the user on how to handle misconduct through escape, confrontation, and de-escalation techniques.

Contact us to schedule your free demo!

Why should I choose Sisu?

Studies show training in virtual reality has increased user retention of information and comprehension, as well as empathy. Most training solutions are passively administered via videos or manuals and have been surveyed to be unengaging.

How does pricing work?

The yearly training costs are $49.99 per user for a non-supervisory license, and $99.99 per user for a supervisory license.

Do I have to provide VR headsets?

Yes, employers are responsible for purchasing VR headsets. We recommend the Pico VR G2 4K.

Is there a non-VR version available?

Yes! A desktop version of our training (compatible with Windows and Mac operating systems) is available.

What if my workplace is remote?

We provide recommendations on how to deploy headsets to your remote workforce. We also have a non-VR version of the training available (see answer to Question 5).

Who creates Sisu VR’s content and how is it created?

The training is based on real harassment cases and developed in-house with the help of our team of HR and training expert(s).

Does this training meet certified standards and regulations?

Yes, we meet requirements for all 50 U.S. states.
